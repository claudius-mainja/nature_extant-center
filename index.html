Who We Are

We are an Environmental Learning Centre, a dedicated facility promoting environmental awareness, education, and sustainable practices. We target schools, professionals, and communities in fragile settings to address critical environmental challenges. Through modern professional training programs and hands-on activities, we empower individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to protect our environment.

Our Mission

Empowering individuals through education to become effective environmental stewards

Our Impact

Aligning with SDGs to create meaningful environmental change

Our Approach

Combining theoretical knowledge with practical, hands-on experience
