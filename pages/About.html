About Us.

We are an Environmental Learning Centre , a dedicated learning facility promoting environmental awareness, education, and sustainable practices. The Centre targets schools, professions, communities especially the ones in fragile settings, and local organizations to address environmental challenges such as deforestation, climate change, water scarcity, and waste management which are posing harm to wildlife, people and the environment itself. The Centre offers educative workshops, modern professional intensive training programs, and hands-on activities to empower individuals with knowledge and skills to protect the environment thus it ushers a formal qualification to individuals partaking the highlighted focus areas. We align with Zimbabwe’s national environmental policies and the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), particularly SDG 13 (Climate Action), SDG 15 (Life on Land), and SDG 4 (Quality Education).
